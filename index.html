This tool was created by Kevin Cannon to help people understand the changes made to the network through the different rounds of consultations. The original plan was published end of 2018 and there have been a few revisions based on community feedback since then.

  • Original The bus network as it existed in 2019.
  • V1 The initial draft put out to consultation
  • V2 Major changes based on the consultation
  • V3 Small changes based on the consultation
  • V4 As of 2023 the latest version of the network that will exist when everything is finished.

Note: I didn't make these maps and there are some small inaccuracies that I'm unable to fix, so always check busconnects.ie for official info.

Apologies I don't have the maps extending further to the outskirts, but I was limited by the maps I had available to me.
